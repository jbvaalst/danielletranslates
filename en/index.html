ABOUT ME

I published illustrated (school) planners for many years. I worked my way up from junior product manager to senior, then on to publisher. For a long time our collection consisted of paper products, in the last few years we added a digital version. I absolutely loved my job. I worked with quite a small team so my work was very diverse, I was sort of a jack-of-all-trades.

Those trades included travelling the world, sourcing brands for our collections, convincing licensors, negotiating contracts, visiting and exhibiting at international fairs, guiding our sales team and selling to major retailers. I also briefed designers, wrote editorials and got to work on our marketing and communication. In the past few years I also gained some knowledge on the development of native apps, the App Store, Play Store and Google Analytics.

But when the world came to a halt I realized: this does not work anymore, I need a change, a new challenge. A bit scary, but also freeing in a way. The world at my feet, all options open.

My background

Because of my background I have gained experience in many different fields. A brief look into my professional life:

2018 - 2022

Co-founder & co-owner AgenDO! BV - Woerden

AgenDO!, an illustrated school and year round planner application for mobile phones. The app combined practical digital planner features with old school fun from branded illustrated paper planners. A perfect marketingtool to ensure a brand is seen multiple times a day by the target group.

2005 – 2007

Co-founder & co-owner Individualz Inc. - New York City, USA

The special Dutch illustrated planners went USA! A publishing company for school planners and stationery, especially for the American market. We created a tailor made collection for the US, including some very special American licenses.

2001 – 2022

Publisher Paper Works BV - Woerden

A publishing company for school planners and stationery. The collection contained only the very best brands in each segment. In addition to the licensed collection we designed and produced private label collections for large retail chains.

1991 – 2001

Senior product manager Trax Company BV - Woerden

A publishing company for school planners, year round planners and stationery. The demand was high, the number of publishers small. Pioneering with new titles, organizing events around the planners. Learned a lot!

AMERICAN ADVENTURE!

My ambition to conquer the world with our illustrated planners together with my fascination for the United States resulted in the founding of an American based company. An incredible challenge. We got to use an office on 1 Rockefeller Plaza, that by itself made it all worth it. We got to conduct meetings with fashion brands and large magazine publishers, close license deals, design and sell products exclusively for the US market. I’m still so proud of our first ever exhibit in the US, at the largest stationery show in the US. We met with scouts, large buyers and owners of mom-and-pop stores.

It was an incredible adventure and it brought me so much. Not in the least a lot of experiences which I can now use for my tailor made translations. And hopefully help you out, translating and finetuning your content for the Dutch market. If you prefer me to write the perfect English text for your Dutch customers, I love to do that as well. That´s a trade I picked up during my many years working with and in the US.
